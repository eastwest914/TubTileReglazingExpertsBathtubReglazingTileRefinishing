FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about bathtub reglazing, tile refinishing and our services across New York City.

What is bathtub reglazing?

Bathtub reglazing (also called refinishing or resurfacing) is a process that restores a worn, damaged or outdated bathtub to a like-new appearance. A licensed technician cleans the surface, repairs any chips or cracks, applies a bonding agent and sprays a professional-grade topcoat. The result looks and feels brand new at a fraction of the replacement cost. Most NYC jobs are completed the same day.

Why choose reglazing over replacement in NYC?

There is no reason to replace any fixture in your home or office unless it is completely broken. Surface flaws can be corrected through reglazing at a fraction of the replacement cost. Bathtub replacement in NYC can cost $2,000 to $6,000 or more, while reglazing is completed in a few hours with no construction mess and no major disruption to your daily routine.

Are your technicians licensed?

Yes. All of our technicians are trained, experienced and licensed to perform bathtub reglazing and tile refinishing work in New York City. We take the time to properly prepare surfaces before the process begins, work carefully and efficiently, and clean up completely once the job is done. You can have complete confidence in the professionalism and quality of our team.

What services do you offer in NYC?

We offer bathtub refinishing, tile refinishing, bathtub restoration, bathtub reglazing, tub refinishing, tub glazing, wall tile reglazing, tile glazing, sink reglazing, cabinet refinishing and shower reglazing. We work on porcelain, ceramic, fiberglass and acrylic surfaces throughout all five NYC boroughs. We can also change the color of your fixtures during the reglazing process.

How long before I can use my tub after reglazing?

We recommend waiting 24 hours after reglazing before using your bathtub. This allows the coating to fully cure and harden for maximum durability and a flawless finish. Our technician will provide you with a full set of care instructions after each job to help you maintain the beautiful new finish for as long as possible.
